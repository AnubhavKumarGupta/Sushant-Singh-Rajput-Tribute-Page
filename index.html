1986 - 2020

  • Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor who gained prominence in the Bollywood film industry. Born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar, he had a passion for acting from a young age and pursued it earnestly. Rajput started his career as a television actor, appearing in popular shows like "Pavitra Rishta," where he gained widespread recognition and a loyal fanbase.
  • In 2013, Sushant made his Bollywood debut with the film "Kai Po Che!" directed by Abhishek Kapoor, which was well-received by both critics and audiences. He went on to deliver notable performances in movies like "Shuddh Desi Romance," "PK," and "Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!" However, it was his portrayal of former Indian cricket captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni in the biopic "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story" that catapulted him to stardom and earned him widespread acclaim.
  • Sushant Singh Rajput was known for his versatile acting skills, charming persona, and dedication to his craft. He was praised for his ability to bring depth and authenticity to his characters, making them relatable and memorable. Despite facing challenges and setbacks in his personal life and career, he continued to pursue his passion for acting with determination and resilience.
  • Tragically, Sushant Singh Rajput passed away on June 14, 2020, 34 at the age of , leaving behind a legacy of memorable performances and a void in the hearts of his fans and the entertainment industry. His untimely demise shocked the nation and sparked discussions about mental health awareness and the pressures of fame in the film industry. Sushant's contributions to Indian cinema will be remembered and cherished for years to come.
